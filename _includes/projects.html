Stereoendoscopy

  • Stereo-reconstruction, OpenCV, C++

Stereoendoscopy is a promising new technology that enables surgeons to view the surface of the mitral valve in real-time- the data from the stereo-camera can be used for reconstructing the depth of the images. This is of a huge advantage to the surgeons, as it helps them to understand otherwise unseen geometries and hence use this information to make better surgical decisions.
